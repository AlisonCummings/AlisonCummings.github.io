Alison Cummings worked in politics and government for about a decade, from staffing various state and congressional campaigns to a career at the NYS Legislature. Her political interests include environmental policy and rewilding initiatives, developing community outreach strategies, and civics education. She received a degree in Toxicology from St. John's University in NY, and is currently learning to code. Pursuing a Professional Certificate through MIT's Fullstack Development with MERN course, she's found that solving a coding problem is more satisfying than watching the Giants win. She knits sweaters she doesn't wear, and enjoys assembling flat-pack furniture for friends and colleagues.
Alison is based in Queens, New York and can be reached via email or on LinkedIn.
